About Soccer

Soccer, also known as football around the world, is one of the most popular sports ever played. The game has existed for hundreds of years, with early versions appearing in places like China, Greece, and Rome. But the modern form of soccer that we know today started in England in the 1800s when the first official rules were created.

The goal of the game is simple: score more goals than the other team. Each team has 11 players, including the goalkeeper, and they play on a large field trying to move the ball without using their hands. The sport is fast, competitive, and takes a mix of skill, strategy, teamwork, and endurance.
